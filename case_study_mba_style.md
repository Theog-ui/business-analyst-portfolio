# Case Study: Raising First‑Pass Quality While Cutting Turnaround

**Context**: Distributed content‑ops team across brands; approvals lagging; QA inconsistent.  
**Intervention**: Implemented two‑step QA + rubric; standardized TSV/JSON outputs; weekly status cadence and risk log; automated reporting.  
**Results (8 weeks)**: ~35% faster TAT; FPAR from ~62% → ~86%; clearer stakeholder decisions.  
**Artifacts**: `scenarios.tsv`, `qa_rubric.md`, `qa_log.tsv`, `metrics.csv`, `metrics_trends.png`.
